about

    Based in New York, NY, we’re looking to build the first free to use audiobook streaming platform with commercial content. Users will be able to search our online database, play and pause audiobooks at their leisure across all platforms, and store and manage their unfinished books on a ‘shelf’, all while encountering ads at regular intervals.

    We intend to integrate narrus with various social networks, allowing users to post on media outlets what they’re currently reading, as well as the various ‘achievements’ they’ll acquire based on what they’ve read. Our dream is to promote an international climate of literature, where any party in the world (with online access) can, as a community, rekindle love for the art of narrative and our oral tradition.
