BLACKBODY RADIATION DEMO
  • Click on the thermometer to set the temperature (K).
  • The graph will change, showing what distribution of light spectra are emitted from a perfect blackbody at that temperature.
  • Use the black sliders to select a range of wavelengths (nm).
NOTE: The graph you see here is logarithmically scaled. When you select a range of wavelengths, click ZOOM to see a linearly scaled section of the graph.
