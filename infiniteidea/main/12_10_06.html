I want to see more music videos like this, hands down.
    A mini-cinematic, short film set to music. It's not musicians wandering about aimlessly with their guitars, or dancing about in whatever scraps they could find in their garbage can, but a serious marriage of typical cinema conventions with good music. Granted, this specific example of such a music video is a bit 'interpretable', meaning, it's blatantly 'about' something, which makes it a little psuedo-intellectual to me. It feels forced, in the way that it's the kind of film that anyone can stand up and easily proclaim 'I KNOW WHAT THIS IS ABOUT' and that type of story doesn't CHALLENGE anyone. Still, it is quite good, and I suggest you look into the film's director, goes by the name of  'Nabil'  . He's a little bit too instagram-happy for my liking but, hey, he's got a good grasp of aesthetic. MY OPINIONS ARE INTERESTING AND ABSOLUTE
