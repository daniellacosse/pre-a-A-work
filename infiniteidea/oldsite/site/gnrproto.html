Gallatin News Report Prototype Episode - March 2010

I uploaded this in LQ 'cause it's a prototype.

Basically this was a run through of the process to see what we need to make this presentable;

1. A body mic - The sound quality REALLY needs to be improved, that's the most glaring flaw.
2. De-interlacing - I can get a thing to do it this weekend. It may just be Facebook though I'll need to check.
3. SPEAK SLOWER. This extra time would give the ticker tape a slower pace as well.
4. Tickertape needs to cut out during non-diagetic sequences. It's just distracting.
5. Graphics need to be slightly re-arranged for readability.
6. A HIGHER TELEPROMPTER!!!
7. The end also cuts out too soon. That's my bad.
8. Makeup would be good too.
9. A smoother wipe on the initial graphic transition. I'll have to engineer that in AE.

That's all I can find at the moment. Please leave any other improvements you see in the comments area. Once we get the flow down we can focus on writing better material.
