Master Builder - August 2009

A lazy summer day with a friend... boredom led us to produce yet another satire; this time about a the perils of being a contractor. Don't let its dark tone fool you; it's supposed to be funny. Unfortunately we were pretty much the only ones to find it so amusing, but I feel it is worth posting nonetheless. It was my first attempt at ADR and Foley, soon to be followed by newer, more successful attempts. It also took advantage of my new toys, and actually looks halfway decent.
