Sight and Sound Film- Summer 2010

I have quite a lot to say about these films, so I leave that part up to the YouTube descriptions.

Sight and Sound Film is an NYU: Tisch class where students are expected to get into crews of four and make twenty films in six weeks. Each student writes, directs and edits five of those films.
